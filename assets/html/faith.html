My Beliefs

Explore a some of my Beliefs, including my faith and my values.

My Faith

    I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. I was baptized at the age of 8 by my father, Francisco Souza.

    I served as a missionary from 2005 to 2007 in the beautiful city of Rio de Janeiro.

    I married my wife Fernanda Souza in 2008 at the Curitiba Temple. Our sons, Pedro, André and Tiago, chose to be baptized at the age 8, in 2018, 2020 and 2023.

    All my greatest joys are associated with my family and my faith.

    I believe that we are children of God. I believe that he sent Jesus Christ to pay for our sins. I believe that God and Jesus Christ live and that they guide their church through prophets. These beliefs were developed through sincere search followed by simple but undeniable confirmations from the Holy Spirit.

    For more information, visit www.comeuntochrist.org

My Values

    Spirituallity

    Keeping a close relationship with God. This is the foundation for a successful life and career. Spirituality will help me feel accountable for my choices and have guidance for better choices.

    Charity

    Being grateful and serving others without looking for a reward. By looking for excellence I will have an improved ability to give back and serve others.

    Integrity

    Caring and striving to be a role model for my family. My family is a powerful and easy-to-remember motivation for me to do the right thing every time and everywhere.

    Exellence

    Looking for excellence in all areas of life, including ethics, through hard work and consistency. This broad view of excellence cannot be a reality unless I live with integrity.
